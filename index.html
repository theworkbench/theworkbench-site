TheWorkben.ch

A creative coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney

The Workbench is a coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney, that’s home to creative people doing incredible things. We believe that working together is better, so we’re creating a place where you can work and be surrounded by other creative people.

We’re designers, developers, writers, artists and business people.

We love collaboration and seeing what happens when great people pour their passion into projects.

Come show us what you’re working on.

Drop in: Level 2, 645 Harris Street, Ultimo. Turn right out of the lift and follow the corridor all the way around. We’re the last door on the left.

Get in touch: Email / Twitter / Blog

The space

Our Ultimo, Sydney, workspace is in a light-filled heritage building and is fitted out with ergonomic desks, a gorgeous Memphis Red workbench runs the length of the room and if you’d like to try a standing desk there’s another bench that’s the perfect height for that too. More relaxed? There’s plenty of space on the sofas by the window.

But we know the best ideas don’t always happen at your desk, so there’s also a well-equipped kitchen.

Typical opening hours are 8.30am - 5pm, Monday to Friday.

Photos of the space can be found on our blog.

Membership

Anyone is welcome to drop in to use the space and meet the great bunch of people who call The Workbench home. Our drop-in rate is $40/day.

Once you’ve worked here for a day, we can roll the cost of that day towards your first month of membership.

  • Basic membership

    $40/month

    If you’re coming in for more than one day a month, this is a great way to be a part of the community. Basic members get one day free a month, access to our events and a cheaper rate for additional days.

  • Lite membership

  • Full membership

    $500/month

Additional days

Basic and Lite members can add additional days for $30/day.

All memberships include access to our internal mailing list, so you’re always a part of what’s happening in our community and hear about all the upcoming events.

If you want to talk or ask some questions, we like emails at hello@theworkben.ch, but we love to answer questions in person.
