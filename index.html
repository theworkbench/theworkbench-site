TheWorkben.ch

A creative coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney

TheWorkben.ch is a coworking space in Sydney that’s home to creative people doing incredible things. We believe that working together is better, so we're creating a place where you can work and be surrounded by other creative people.

We’re designers, developers, writers, artists and business people.

We love collaboration and seeing what happens when great people pour their passion into projects.

Come show us what you’re working on.

Drop in: Level 2, 645 Harris Street, Ultimo. Turn right out of the lift and follow the corridor all the way around. We’re the last door on the left.

The space

Our Ultimo workspace is in a light-filled heritage building and is fitted out with ergonomic desks, a gorgeous Memphis Red workbench runs the length of the room and if you’d like to try a standing desk there’s another bench that’s the perfect height for that too. More relaxed? There’s plenty of space on the sofas by the window.

But we also know the best ideas don’t always happen at your desk, so there’s also a well-equipped kitchen.

Photos of the space can be found on our blog.

Membership

From August 13 (once our free launch period ends) anyone is welcome to drop in to use the space and meet the great bunch of people who call TheWorkben.ch home. Our drop-in rate is $40/day.

Once you’ve worked here for a day, we can roll the cost of that day towards your first month of Basic membership.

  • Basic membership

    $40/month

    If you’re coming in for more than one day a month, this is a great way to be a part of the community. Basic members get one day free a month, access to our events and a cheaper rate for additional days.

  • Lite membership

Additional days

Basic and Lite members can add additional days for $30/day.

All our memberships include access to our basecamp and internal mailing list, so you’re always a part of the what’s going on in our community and upcoming events.
