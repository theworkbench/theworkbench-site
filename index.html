We’re a coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney.

The Workbench is a space where you can work and be surrounded by the best coworkers: other creative people, making interesting things.

Today people can work from anywhere, which means they sometimes don't have anywhere to work from. We’re about fixing that. It’s not about sharing desks and offices, it’s about a creative community.

We’re designers, writers, developers, artists and business people.

The space

Our Ultimo, Sydney, workspace is on the second floor of a light-filled heritage warehouse and is fitted out with ergonomic desks and a gorgeous Memphis Red workbench which runs the length of the room.

If you’d like to try a standing desk there’s another bench that’s the perfect height for that too. More relaxed? There’s beanbags and plenty of space on the sofas by the window.

We know ideas don’t always happen at your desk.

Take a break in our well-equipped kitchen, take five on an old-school video game arcade table or take a walk in the park right behind our building.

The space is bathed in super-fast wireless internet and there’ also gigabit ethernet to most of the desks if you prefer your internet wired.

We share dedicated mens and womens bathrooms with other offices on our floor, and there’s room to park your bike next to your desk.

Typical opening hours are 9am - 5pm, Monday to Friday. Full-time members have the option to have keys to access the space 24/7.

Photos of the space can be found on our blog.

Ultimo, Sydney is our ‘hood

We’re excited to be a part of the community in Ultimo, just outside the Sydney CBD.

The area has plenty of cafes (Mio Mia in our building, Le Depot just up the road, and a stack more including the coffee-nerd haven Mecca Espresso) and plenty of options for lunch (we’re often at Kopitiam for malaysian street food). Haymarket’s a few minutes walk if you’d like Sydney’s best noodles, featuring incongrous grapevines

There’s a park right behind our block that’s perfect for soaking up sunshine or kicking a ball around at lunch, and the Ian Thorpe Acquatic Centre is five minutes away if swimming laps or hitting the sauna is more your style.
