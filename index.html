TheWorkben.ch

A creative coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney

TheWorkben.ch is a coworking space in Sydney that’s home to creative people doing incredible things.

We love collaboration and seeing what happens when great people pour their passion into projects.

Come show us what you’re working on.

Drop in: Level 2, 645 Harris Street, Ultimo. Turn right out of the lift and follow the corridor all the way around. We're the last door on the left.

Get in touch: Email / Twitter / Tumblr

The space

Our Ultimo workspace is in a light-filled heritage building and is fitted out with ergonomic desks, a gorgeous Memphis Red workbench runs the length of the room and there’s plenty of space to relax by the window.

But we also know the best ideas don’t always happen at your desk, so there’s also a well-equipped kitchen.

Photos of the space can be found on our blog.

How it works

Until August 10, there is no charge to work at TheWorkben.ch.

Come by and try it out, without cost, to see how much you love it.

Want to drop in for a day to hang out with a great bunch of people who are making interesting things? Easy! Just walk in.

No bookings. No reservations. No calling ahead.

If you want to talk or ask some questions, we like emails at hello@theworkben.ch, but we love to answer questions in person.
