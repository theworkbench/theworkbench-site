TheWorkben.ch

Coworking space in Ultimo, Sydney

TheWorkben.ch is a coworking space in Sydney that’s home to creative people doing incredible things on the web.

We love collaboration and seeing what happens when great people pour their passion into projects.

Come show us what you’re working on.

Drop in: Level 2, 645 Harris Street, Ultimo. Turn right out of the lift and follow the corridor all the way around. We're the last door on the left.

Get in touch: Email / Twitter / Tumblr

The space

Our Ultimo workspace is in a light-filled heritage building and is fitted out with ergonomic desks, a collaborative workbench runs the length of the room and there’s plenty of space to relax by the window.

But we also know the best ideas don’t always happen at your desk, so there’s also a well-equipped kitchen.

And we'll have beer on tap! Once we're appropriately licensed, of course.
